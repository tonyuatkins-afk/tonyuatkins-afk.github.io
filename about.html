About

Tony Atkins is an electronics builder based in the Portland / Beaverton area of Oregon.

Previously at Biamp Systems, working on professional audio hardware with deep experience in SMT assembly, circuit design, and manufacturing. Hands-on with everything from schematic capture through production troubleshooting.

Barely Booting is the project that puts that experience into open-source hardware for vintage computing. The current focus is NetISA, an ISA expansion card that gives pre-Pentium PCs a working path to the modern internet. Future projects follow the same pattern: new hardware, built carefully, for old computers that deserve to keep running.

Everything is open source. Schematics, firmware, CPLD logic, documentation, build logs, test suites. The goal is for anyone to be able to build one.
